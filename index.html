Hello! My name is August Murphy-Beach. I am currently attending Gonzaga University, studying Computer Science, Leadership, and Spanish. With this website, I hope to hone my web development skills, and have fun doing it! My passions are fitness, computers, and tennis.

My most recent model, Hillclimbr, allows the user to get a real depiction of how fast they'd be racing the greats in cycling. I hope to export Hillclimbr to a mobile application, in order to further develop my skills with computers.
